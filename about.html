Our Mission

To support children, teens, and young adults of Sonoma County focusing on mental health awareness and suicide prevention.

About The Zane Templin Foundation

The Zane Templin Foundation is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization founded in memory of Zane Walter Cruz Templin. Zane was 24 years old in 2016 when he lost his life to suicide.
