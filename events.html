Annual High Heels for Hope

High Heels For Hope benefits The Zane Templin Foundation, a nonprofit 501(c)(3) focusing on suicide awareness and prevention serving the children, teens, and young adults in Sonoma County. Together we can make a difference. We can save lives and bring hope to those affected by suicide. High Heels for Hope is an evening of glamour and fun where the women put on their best heels, dine on wine country cuisine, enjoy sonoma county wines, while dancing the night away.
