Our Community

We want to help!

Our goal is to meet needs locally here in the community where we see them, whenever we are able. If you see a need, let us know. We can only help if we know where we are needed. Please email us and let us know as much as you can about the situation, what the specific needs are, the timeframe, and how we can reach you, and if possible the person in need. Please email us at zanetemplinfoundation@gmail.com.
